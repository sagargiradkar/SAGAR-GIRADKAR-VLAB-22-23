INTRODUCTION

The DSP Virtual Laboratory is an initiative of Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Govt. of India,
under the National Mission on Education through Information and Communication Technology (NME-ICT).
Laboratory courses on hands-on experiments are an integral part of engineering education. The content
of this website aims to provide a virtual laboratory platform for undergraduate Engineering students
studying the course of Digital Signal Processing.
